I'm Roshan a DevSecOps Engineer and Site Reliability Engineer based in Chicago, Illinois, with 6+ years of experience keeping large-scale cloud platforms secure, stable, and fast.

My career has taken me through some of the most demanding production environments in the country from TransUnion's regulated fintech infrastructure to Dollar General's massive retail supply chain, and Procter & Gamble's mission-critical enterprise systems.

I believe security shouldn't slow teams down. I embed DevSecOps controls directly into delivery pipelines so that shipping fast and shipping safe aren't a trade-off. When systems break at 2am and they do I'm the person on-call who makes them not break again.

Beyond engineering, I actively publish research at the intersection of AI and cloud security. 5 peer-reviewed papers covering AI-driven compliance, multi-cloud governance, and predictive security systems.
